About Me

I'm just a simple racoon trying to make it in this cold, harsh world. I was born in a nondescript woodland near the edge of a Texan highway and went to college at the University of Maryland where I received my bachelors in the ABCs. Since then, I've been working full-time at Facebook as 'entity who digs through dumpsters in the company parking lot'. I'm extremely passionate about my work and think of it as a vocation rather than a job.

Here are some things that set my soul on fire in order of importance:

  1. Eating garbage
  2. Stoic philosophy
  3. Running this side business
  4. Hot chowder
  5. The dulcet tones of Irish songstress Enya
